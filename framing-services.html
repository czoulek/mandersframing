Framing Services

Selection, design and craftsmanship are key to everything we do. We can help you finish your masterpiece.

Huge Selection

The average frame shop carries 600-700 frame moulding samples. We have over 1,200 moulding samples in store to choose from. We offer a wide selection of wood, metal and polystyrene frames from multiple suppliers.

Each year we visit with our sales reps and add some of the latest offerings on the market. We keep our moulding selection current with the trends in interior design. If you are looking for something modern, ornate, trendy or timeless, we can help you.

Are you a DIYer?

If you need matboard or foam core to frame your own artwork, we can help. Check out the supplies we have so you can do it yourself »

Design Process

Custom framing can be overwhelming with all of the choices available to you. We will listen to your thoughts and provide you with choices to help realize your vision. We can help with frame choice, matboard, colors, glass selection and other artistic embellishments.

Whether you are looking for something simple or with that pop of color, we can help. Color combinations are endless, so if you can imagine it, we should be able to create it.

Quality Craftsmanship

Our work is done on site. We will take great care of your artwork or family heirloom. You can have peace of mind that the same people who helped you make your frame choices are the same people completing the work.

We use Tru-Vue brand conservation grade glass. It offers 99% UV protection to help resist fading. This, in conjunction with acid free or archival matboards, will help preserve your piece for generations to come.

Our work is guaranteed. If there is a defect in the materials or workmanship, please let us know and we will take care of the issue to your complete satisfaction.

We understand that accidents happen. If the glass breaks on a picture that we framed, just bring it in with our sticker on the back. We will only charge you for the materials used, with no labor charge.

We Can Frame...

If you can imagine it, we can find a way to frame it. With a variety of challenges we have come across in the past and with the support of our suppliers, we are confident that we will be able to provide a solution for whatever you can throw at us.

From photos to certificates, jerseys, medals, artifacts and instruments, we've seen just about everything. No job is too big or too small. Bring your thoughts and ideas in and we can explore the possibilities.
