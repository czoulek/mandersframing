Fine Art, Prints, Children's Art

We can help you pick out the proper type and color matboard to accentuate the mood and tone of your piece. Whether a single or double mat, pop of color or a texture, we can make the picture look the best it possibly can.

Photographs and Posters

Mounting your photos and posters to foam core is an option to minimize any waves or wrinkles that occur from being rolled and transported.

Cross-stitch, Needlepoint, Embroidery

We can stretch your cross-stitch, needlework or embroidery. Whether you decide on batting without glass or matting and glass, your options are endless.

Shadow Boxes, Keepsakes, Sports Memorabelia

For these non-traditional options, we use acid free mounting and sewing supplies to ensure that your items retain their value over time.

Canvas, Oils and Mirrors

We can stretch your canvas for gallery wrap or traditional framing. Existing mirrors can be framed or we can order custom sizes to fit your space. They can be flat or beveled and come in different finishes.
