Framing Supplies

Matboard

Matboards from Crescent and Bainbridge are available for purchase. Come in and browse the specifiers to find exactly what you need.

Foam core

Foam core is available for purchase.

  • Thickness: 3/16" and 1/2"
  • Sizes: 20" x 30" to 48" x 96"
