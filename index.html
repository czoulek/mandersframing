Why Choose Manders Picture Framing?

Manders Framing is family-owned and operated in Milwaukee since 1957. Our goal is to provide quality picture framing at a fair price. We have a large selection of moulding to choose from to help you find the look you want.

Custom framing isn't something you do every day and we understand that. We are here to help you through the design process and answer any questions you may have. Your artwork or family heirloom means a lot to you. We're honored that you entrust us to frame it and we want you to be happy with the end result.

Our work is done on site. We take great care of your artwork or family heirloom. You can have peace of mind that the same people who helped you make your frame choices are the same people completing the work.

Our work is guaranteed. If there is a defect in the materials or workmanship, please let us know and we will take care of the issue to your complete satisfaction.
